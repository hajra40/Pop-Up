Happy Engineer's Day

Why do we celebrate?

15th September is the birthday of the great Engineer of India Sir M Visveswaraya. The day is celebrated as Engineers day in India inorder to honor his achievements. He was the 19th diwan of Mysore, the chief Engineer of the construction of the Krishna Raja Sagar dam of Karnataka and also for the flood management system in Hyderabad. One of the largest universities of India Visveswaraya Tecchnological University, Belagavi also has been named in his honour and also the Govt. of India honoured him with Bharat Ratna.
